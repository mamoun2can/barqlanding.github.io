The right carrier with the right load

The Future of Freight Procurement

Easily find, procure, move and track in-land transportation


Barq is an online transportation marketplace connecting shippers to carriers, and ultimately matching the right load to the right carrier. Barq acts as an enabler for inland transportaiton carriers, supporting their businesses generate analytics to choose the right load, and offer a greater service level. Shippers and freight forwarders benefit from Barq's open marketplace ensuring the price and service level are aligned. By streamlining freight procurement, Barq provides greater effeciency while increasing visibility in the transportation chain.
